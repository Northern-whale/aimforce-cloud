## Tax & Legal Considerations

### Important:

1. **Business Structure**
   - Sole proprietorship (simplest, default)
   - OR LLC (liability protection, professional)
   - Consult accountant for best choice

2. **Track ALL Income**
   - Separate business bank account
   - Use QuickBooks, FreshBooks, or spreadsheet
   - Save all receipts for expenses

3. **Deductible Expenses**
   - Equipment (camera, microphone, computer)
   - Software subscriptions (editing, ElevenLabs)
   - Music licensing
   - Props and products for videos
   - Portion of home office (if applicable)
   - Mileage to/from filming locations
   - Professional services (editor, thumbnail designer)

4. **Quarterly Estimated Taxes**
   - Once earning $600+/year, pay quarterly taxes
   - Avoid penalties at year-end
   - Set aside 25-30% of income for taxes

5. **1099 Forms**
   - YouTube/Google sends 1099 if earn $600+
   - Affiliate partners send 1099s
   - Keep all forms for tax filing

**Recommendation:** Consult with accountant once earning $500+/month
