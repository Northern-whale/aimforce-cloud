## â“ FAQ

**Q: Can I really make $1,000/month from this?**  
A: Yes, but it takes 10-14 months of consistent effort (2 uploads/week). The monetization plan shows realistic projections.

**Q: What if I'm not good at editing?**  
A: The production guide is designed for beginners. CapCut has auto-editing features. Start simple, improve gradually.

**Q: How much time does this take per week?**  
A: About 6-8 hours total: 2 hours filming, 1 hour scripting, 4-5 hours editing, 1 hour publishing/engagement.

**Q: Do I need Tulsi (an actual dog)?**  
A: No! The "character" is AI-voiced. You just film the pet store environment and products. Any dog footage works.

**Q: Can this work for businesses outside Denver?**  
A: Absolutely! Local angle actually helps (less competition). Just adjust location tags in SEO.

**Q: What if my business doesn't have pets?**  
A: See `Scale-to-Other-Businesses.md` - this works for bookstores, coffee shops, nurseries, bakeries, and more!
