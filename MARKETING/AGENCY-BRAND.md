**Primary Colors:**

- **Electric Cyan (#06B6D4)** - Energy, innovation, tech

- **Success Green (#10B981)** - Positive outcomes, growth

**Body:**
- **Inter** (consistent throughout) or

## 7. Brand Voice & Messaging

### Voice Characteristics

**We Sound:**
- **Clear** - No jargon unless necessary, explain complex simply
- **Confident** - We know what we're doing, but we're not arrogant
- **Practical** - Focus on outcomes, not technology for its own sake
- **Approachable** - Professional but human, not corporate-stiff

**We Don't Sound:**
- Corporate buzzword soup ("synergize", "leverage", "paradigm")
- Overly technical ("our transformer-based NLP model...")
- Hype-y ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "disrupting")
- Salesy ("limited time offer!", "act now!")

"We build AI that actually works for your business."

## 10. Brand Guidelines (Quick Reference)

### Logo Usage
- Minimum size: 120px width (digital), 1" (print)
- Clear space: Equal to height of "T" on all sides
- Don't: Rotate, distort, outline, add effects

### Color Codes

**Primary:**
- Deep Blue: `#1E3A8A` (RGB: 30, 58, 138)
- Electric Cyan: `#06B6D4` (RGB: 6, 182, 212)
- White: `#FFFFFF` (RGB: 255, 255, 255)

**Accents:**
- Success Green: `#10B981`
- Warning Amber: `#F59E0B`
- Neutral Gray: `#6B7280`

### Typography
- Headings: Inter Bold, 32-56px
- Subheadings: Inter Semibold, 20-28px
- Body: Inter Regular, 16-18px
- Captions: Inter Regular, 14px

### Writing Style
- Use active voice ("We build AI products" not "AI products are built by us")
- Write in second person when addressing audience ("You can automate..." not "One can automate...")
- Keep sentences short (15-20 words average)
- Use contractions ("we're" not "we are") for approachability
- Avoid jargon unless explaining it
- Start with the benefit, then the feature
